# Privacy Policy for DollarBucks Dash

**Last Updated**: [1/8/2026]

## Introduction

DollarBucks Dash ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

DollarBucks Dash is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal information
- User names or profiles
- Performance data or statistics
- Device information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Any data that could identify you or your child

## How Our App Works

DollarBucks Dash is a completely offline application. All data created in the app, including:
- Child profiles
- Multiplication practice statistics
- Earned rewards ("Dollar Bucks")
- Learning progress

...is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

DollarBucks Dash does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

DollarBucks Dash is designed for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal information from children collected, stored, or shared.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This data:
- Is only accessible on your device
- Is deleted if you uninstall the app
- Can be backed up through iCloud or iTunes if you have device backups enabled

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:
- Delete profiles directly in the app
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- Manage your device backups through your device settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
